Meet the Artist

Hi, I'm Aleena Uvaise

Founder and freelance beauty artist behind ALN Glamz Makeover Studio. Aleena specialises in Bridal Makeup, Airbrush Makeup, HD Makeup, Glass Skin Makeup, Hijab Styling, Hair Styling, and Saree Draping. Every look is crafted with care, calm attention, and a love for making clients feel confident, elegant, and truly themselves.

From soft natural glam to bold bridal transformations, my work is focused on enhancing your beauty — not hiding it.
